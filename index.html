Academic Programs

Our comprehensive nursing programs are designed to meet diverse educational goals while maintaining rigorous academic standards and extensive clinical training.

General Nursing and Midwifery

3.5 Years

Comprehensive diploma program covering fundamental nursing principles, patient care techniques, and midwifery practices with extensive clinical training.

Eligibility: 10+2 with Science subjects

Bachelor of Science in Nursing

4 Years

Undergraduate degree program providing advanced nursing knowledge, research skills, and leadership preparation for diverse healthcare settings.

Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology

Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing

2 Years

Bridge program for diploma holders seeking degree-level qualifications with advanced nursing concepts and management principles.

Eligibility: GNM diploma with registration

Auxiliary Nurse Midwife

2 Years

Foundational program focusing on basic nursing care and midwifery services, particularly for community and rural healthcare settings.

Eligibility: 10th standard completion
